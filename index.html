How it works

How to complete your first assessment, attend classes, monitor your progress and post your certificate to your LinkedIn profile.

1

Schedule your assessment

Get started by scheduling your first assessment.
Choose your teacher, the time and date that is convenient for you.

Schedule your assessment

2

Complete the pre-assessment quiz

To assess your grammar and writing skills, your assessor needs you to complete a grammar quiz and write a short essay before the scheduled assessment.

3

Attend the assessment

Join your assessor in the virtual classroom at the scheduled time. During the 25-minute session, answer the questions and your assessor will create an assessment report.

4

Review your assessment report

Your assessment report will include a score per criteria and recommendations from your assessor.

5

Add your Certificate to your LinkedIn profile

Certification helps you set yourself apart from other applicants and increases your profile or CV’s visibility.

LinkedIn has confirmed that you can boost you profile views up to 6x by adding your certified skills (view example of the certificate).

Optional

Ready to reach the next level of English Proficiency?
Signup for a plan according to your schedule.

6

Select a plan

Choose from three different plans depending on your learning preferences (1-on-1 or group) and how quickly you want to reach the next level of English Proficiency.

Select a plan

7

Attend the online lessons

Your teacher will contact you according to your predefined schedule. Review the pre-assigned class content, complete the pre-lesson quiz and enter the virtual classroom from your student account to meet with your teacher. Your teacher will send you a class report and homework after each lesson.

8

Review your progress report

Monitor your progress, per assessment, and per criteria from the progress report page. Get to know what you should be working on to upgrade your certificate.
